  • What is a Cheerluck page?

    We have developed a space online that will allow love ones and friends to send cheer and luck through a collage of videos, pictures, and comments! The cheerluck page will be a congregation of online love and support for that person!

  • Who is Cheerluck for?

    For anyone! We feel that Cheerluck works well for someone who is overseas, recovering or just seperated given some circumstances.

  • How to make a Cheerluck?

    It's easy! Just go to the create page at the top of the page!

  • Harassment?

    To combat online harrassment we give the user these layers of protection:

    • The ability to make the Cheerluck page public or private.
    • Allow the creator of the page to verify each post before making the post available to see.
      • We belive that these features will prevent attacks from various online trolls and keep our cheer pages full of cheer and luck!


Who we are!


We are four Computer Science students from Bridgewater State University! We are located between Plymouth and Boston Massachusetts. This idea was born from seeing multiple Reddit posts about sending cards to sick children. We thought having an online space could be an easier and more interactive way for people of all ages to send their love and wishes across the world.
